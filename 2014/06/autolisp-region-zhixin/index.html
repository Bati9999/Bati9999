AutoLISP程序：标识面域的质心

By bati9999

2014-06-12 更新日期:2015-06-12

文章目录

说明：定义命令pcen，用当前的点样式在选中的面域的质心位置画点，效果如图。

;-------begin------
;author：bati@818cad.com
;function: pcen--to add  a point at Centroid of selected region
(defun c:pcen (/ ss entR entAx zcc zcv ptc)
(vl-load-com)
(setq ss (ssget ":S" '((0 . "REGION"))))
(if ss
   (progn
    (setq entR (ssname ss 0))
    (setq entAx (vlax-ename->vla-object entR)) 
    (setq zcc (vlax-variant-value(vlax-get-property entAx "Centroid")))
    (setq zcv (vlax-safearray->list zcc)) 
    (setq ptc (append zcv (list 0.0)))  
    (command "._point" ptc)
   )
)  
(princ)
)
;----------end--------
